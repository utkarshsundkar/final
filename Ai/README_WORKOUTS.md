# Daily Morning Kickstart Workouts

This document lists the exercise routines for the "Morning Kickstart" program, categorized by experience level and day of the week.

## Beginner Plan

### Day 1 - Monday (Upper Body + Core)
**Warm-up:**
*   Shoulder Press: 1 set • 12 reps
*   Side Bend Left: 1 set • 10 reps
*   Side Bend Right: 1 set • 10 reps
*   Shoulder Taps: 1 set • 15 sec

**Main Workout:**
*   Push-ups: 3 sets • 8-10 reps
*   Shoulder Press: 3 sets • 10-12 reps
*   Shoulder Taps: 3 sets • 20 sec
*   Side Plank: 2 sets/side • 20 sec
*   Side Bend Left: 2 sets • 12 reps
*   Side Bend Right: 2 sets • 12 reps

### Day 2 - Tuesday (Lower Body)
**Warm-up:**
*   Air Squat: 1 set • 12 reps
*   Standing Knee Raise Left: 1 set • 10 reps
*   Standing Knee Raise Right: 1 set • 10 reps
*   Side Lunge Left: 1 set • 6 reps
*   Side Lunge Right: 1 set • 6 reps

**Main Workout:**
*   Air Squat: 3 sets • 12 reps
*   Lunge: 3 sets • 10 reps/leg
*   Side Lunge Left: 2 sets • 8 reps
*   Side Lunge Right: 2 sets • 8 reps
*   Glute Bridge: 3 sets • 12 reps

### Day 3 - Wednesday (Core)
**Warm-up:**
*   Glute Bridge: 1 set • 20 reps
*   Standing Oblique Crunches: 1 set • 12 reps
*   Standing Oblique Crunches (2): 1 set • 10 reps

**Main Workout:**
*   Crunches: 3 sets • 15 reps
*   Glute Bridge: 3 sets • 20 reps
*   Standing Oblique Crunches: 3 sets • 15 reps
*   High Plank: 2 sets • 30 sec
*   Standing Oblique Crunches (2): 2 sets • 12 reps

### Day 4 - Thursday (Cardio + Full Body)
**Warm-up:**
*   Jumping Jacks: 1 set • 40 sec
*   Jumps: 1 set • 30 sec

**Main Workout:**
*   High Knees: 3 sets • 30 sec
*   Jumping Jacks: 3 sets • 40 sec
*   Jumps: 3 sets • 30 sec
*   Skater Hops: 2 sets • 10 reps/side
*   Ski Jumps: 2 sets • 6 reps

### Day 5 - Friday (Mobility)
**Warm-up:**
*   Standing Knee Raise Left: 1 set • 10 reps
*   Standing Knee Raise Right: 1 set • 10 reps
*   Hamstring Mobility: 1 set • 8 reps

**Main Workout:**
*   Squat: 3 sets • 8 reps
*   Standing Knee Raise Left: 2 sets • 12 reps
*   Standing Knee Raise Right: 2 sets • 12 reps
*   Hamstring Mobility: 2 sets • 10 reps
